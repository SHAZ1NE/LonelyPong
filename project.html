Bootcamp 2021: Lonely Pong

My first programming project, concluding the 2 week Programming Bootcamp at City (2021). This Processing project demonstrates a comprehensive understanding of the use of classes and interaction between them. I created a "single-player pong". The paddle is controlled with the w and s keys to move up and down. The ball starts in the middle of the screen and moves left always but it can go up or down using random(). The player will then rebound it against the wall until they miss. Every time they hit the ball against the paddle their score increases by 1.
